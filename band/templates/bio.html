Alec Davidson

Alec was born in February and knew then that he wanted to play bass in a seapunk band. After a couple years of searching, Alec finally found the only other people who knew what seapunk was and his life was forever changed. Alec plays bass and back up vocals (although the mic is usually very quiet) as well as keeps the other members of Joyful Waves entertained with his jokes, that are actually really funny.
Fun Facts: Alec's favorite color is purple, he loves giraffes, and has broken the tour bus twice.

Rick Jamil

Having been born on the sandy shores of Karachi, Pakistan, seapunk was destined to be in Rick's blood. Serving as the saltiest of sailors for half a decade did not help. Rick was doomed. He has been playing the drums for Joyful Waves for as long as he can remember and has not found a way of getting out yet.
Fun Facts: Rick's favorite colour is scarlet and the oceans have carried him to more countries than he can count.
